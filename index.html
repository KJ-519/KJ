Basic Lizard Info

Lizardis the common name used for all squamate reptiles other than snakes. Although this grouping is paraphyletic as some lizards are more closely related to snakes than others.


Now that we know some basic information about lizards and their relations, it's time to list some facts.

Lizard Habitats

  • Cold environments
  • Deep Oceans
  • Ground environments
  • Trees
  • Deserts

Lizard Diets

Carnivore Diets
  1. Mammals
  2. Birds
  3. Other Reptiles
Vegetarian Diets
  1. Leaves
  2. Fruits
  3. Flowers

Lizard Types

  • Geckos
  • Iguanas
  • Agama
  • Gila
  • Skink and many more
Lizard Age and Size
  1. Age Maturity from 18 months to 7 years
  2. Length from .6 inches up to 10 feet
  3. Weight from .004 ounces up to 176 pounds
Lizard Fun Facts
  • Some lizards can store up to 60% of their body fat in their tail
  • Alligator lizards shed their skin in one piece like snakes
  • The Madagascan chameleon has a sticky-tipped tongue that it can shoot out farther than the length of its body.
